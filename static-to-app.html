A Guide to Customizing Learning with AI

Why Go Interactive?

Boost Engagement

Active participation leads to deeper learning and retention.

Support Autonomy

Learners progress at their own pace and explore content freely.

Immediate Feedback

Instant correction helps solidify understanding and build confidence.

4 Steps to Transformation

1

Upload Worksheet

Start by providing your existing material (e.g., a PDF) to your chosen AI tool.

2

Write a Clear Prompt

Instruct the AI on what you want to create. Be specific about the format and interactivity.

3

Customize the Output

Review the AI's creation. Ask for refinements, add a scoring system, or change the visuals.

4

Publish & Share

Generate a shareable link for your new app and distribute it to your learners.

Pedagogy & Ethics

Align with Goals

Ensure activities match learning objectives (e.g., CEFR, GSE). The tech should serve the pedagogy.

Prioritize Accessibility

Check readability, font sizes, and color contrast. Ensure the language level is appropriate.

Use AI Ethically

Be transparent with learners. Be mindful of data privacy, content accuracy, bias, and copyright.
