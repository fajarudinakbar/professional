The Art of Pedagogy in Practice

My philosophy is deeply student-centered. I specialize in high-stakes contexts like Business and Academic English, where learners require targeted, real-world language skills.

A key element of my pedagogy is the use of the Global Scale of English (GSE). I use it as a "mirror" to reflect on my teaching, ensuring activities are precisely matched to proficiency levels.

Case Study: Personalized Intervention

Challenge: Disparate skills (Speaking GSE 55 / Writing GSE 45).

Approach: Challenged speaking with complex mock interviews; supported writing with guided sentence frames.

Outcome: Accelerated, balanced progress in both domains.
